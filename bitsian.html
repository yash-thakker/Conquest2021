Conquest BITSian Track

  • Each team must comprise a majority (50% or more) BITS Pilani students enrolled in or after 2015.
  • The participants can be from any of the 4 BITS Campuses namely Pilani, Goa, Hyderabad, and Dubai.
  • The BITS students must be actively involved in the venture.
  • Prize money will be given as cheque, made in the name of Team leader, who should be a BITS student.
  • A Startup may have cross-campus teams (eg. A team of three can have one member from Pilani campus and 2 members from Goa Campus.)
  • In case the startup is selected in the Top-50 through the Pan-India Conquest track, the startup needs to declare while registering, how they’ll want to continue (via the Pan-India track or via the BITSian track.) It can’t be changed later on.
  • Ventures that have acquired funding from external investor-networks (Angels, VCs) cannot participate.

Teams can be asked for proof regarding the aspects mentioned above at any given time during competition. Any team that fails to do so will be disqualified.
