Success Stories

Visit

Co-winner of Conquest 2017, VISIT is an AI-driven chatbox that analyzes patient symptoms and history, to connect them to right medical professionals. They have received funding from Biz Stone the co-founder of Twitter, Snapdeal and Blue Jeans.

Social Cops

Winner of Conquest 2013, Social cops solves critical problems faced by society using data intelligence. They have been working closely with the government and were recognized as one of India’s top 10 startups by NASSCOM. They were also awarded the Global Social Entrepreneurship Award by Microsoft in 2017.

Thinkerbell Labs

Winner of Conquest 2016, Thinkerbell lab’s flagship project Annie is the world’s first Self-Learning Braille Device for the visually impaired. They are also the 2nd runner up in Global Social Venture Competition 2018 Out of 550 teams from 60+ countries.

Swagene

Co-winner of Conquest 2015, Swagene is changing the face of healthcare by suggesting personalized medication, surgeries, and procedures to regain health based on patient genes. They have been featured by NASSCOM and won the CII Startup of the Year 2014.

GameZop

Gamezop is a destination for casual games where users can play games without downloading apps and also share the games with friends over WhatsApp or Facebook as playable weblinks. It’s Co-founder Yashah Agarwal represented India in Theil summit in 2015. They have raised 2.4 crore INR in seed funding.
